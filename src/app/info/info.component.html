What's this for?

"UNC CS Planner" is meant to be a place to plan out your major/minor in the CS department as well as be a place where you can ask for advice from fellow computer science students. As of right now, that's about it, but feel free to suggest any features you'd like to see!

What are some tips for use?

To tag a comment with any classes that are contained within it, simply add the tag "DEPT_XXX", where DEPT is the four letter abbreviation for the deparment (e.g. COMP for computer science, PHYS for physics, etc.) and XXX is the course number. Doing so will let the post show up on the class' detail view (in the roadmap) as well as in any search with that class.

What are my privacy options?

Currently, you can choose whether to display your profile page for users other than yourself, but that's about it.
